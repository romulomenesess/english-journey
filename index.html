Why learn English ?

English is a Germanic language originating in England and is currently the most widely used international language in the world. It is the official or primary language in countries such as the United States, the United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, and New Zealand, and is also widely used in business, science, technology, and global culture.

Currently, English is considered the common language among people from different countries. It is estimated that more than 1.5 billion people speak it, whether as a native language, a second language, or a foreign language. Mastering English is an essential skill for international communication and access to academic and professional opportunities.
